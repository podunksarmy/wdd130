The Story so Far

For better or for worse, the name H.P. Lovecraft is inseparably connected with the horror genre, and in particular with Eldritch Horror. His works, while at times controversial, helped shape the mythos surrounding modern horror fiction. While he is best known for his Cthulu Mythos, he was a prolific author and poet with over 70 stories to his name. Most of the attention to his works has been paid to the aforementioned mythos, but here we have endeavored to bring some of his other works to the forefront.
